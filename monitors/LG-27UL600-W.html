LG 27UL600-W

Specifications
Work Monitor
Price S$499 (Lazada)
Resolution 3840x2160 (4K)
Refresh Rate 60 Hz
Size 27 inches
Panel Type IPS
Response Time 5 ms
Brightness 350 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio 1000:1(Static)
Adaptive Sync Yes (40-60Hz)

Pros

  • High 4K resolution that is good for office and design work
  • Vibrant colours
  • Wide viewing angles
  • Great for multitasking

Cons

  • Blacks look grey in the dark due to low contrast ratio
  • Stand is tilt-only
