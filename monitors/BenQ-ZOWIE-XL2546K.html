BenQ ZOWIE XL2546K

Specifications
Gaming Monitor (eSports)
Price S$799 (Lazada)
Resolution 1920x1080 (FHD)
Refresh Rate 240 Hz
Size 24.5 inches
Panel Type TN
Response Time 0.5 ms
Brightness 270 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio 1000:1(Static)
Adaptive Sync Yes (48-240Hz)
Adjustable Stand Yes
Ports DisplayPort 1.2, 2x HDMI 2.0

Pros

  • Great for eSports gaming
  • Low input lag
  • Exceptional response time
  • Stand has great ergonomics
  • Excellent motion blur reduction feature (DyAc+)

Cons

  • Narrow viewing angles
  • Blacks look grey in the dark due to low contrast
  • Poor for watching movies or design work
