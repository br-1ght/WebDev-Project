LG 27GN950

Specifications
Gaming Monitor
Price S$1300 (Amazon)
Resolution 3840x2160 (4K)
Refresh Rate 144 Hz
Size 27 inches
Panel Type IPS
Response Time 1 ms
Brightness 420 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio 1000:1(Static)
Adaptive Sync Yes (48-144Hz)
Adjustable Stand Yes
Ports DisplayPort 1.4, 2x HDMI 2.0

Pros

  • High 4K resolution that is good for office and design work
  • Wide colour gamut that provides vibrant colours
  • Excellent response time
  • FreeSync support and G-SYNC compatibility

Cons

  • Adjustable stand lacks swivel option
  • Lacks HDMI 2.1 support if you want to use a PS5 at 4K 120Hz
  • Blacks look grey in the dark due to low contrast ratio
